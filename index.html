Rental Terms

Security Deposit

We ask a security deposit on all bikes or cycling gear you rent. You can make a payment via a debit or credit card. We cannot return you money until the gear is returned.

Damage

If you want to rent a bike or any cycling gear, you will be responsible for it until the time of return. The theft and any possible bike damage are included.

Age Restrictions

If you want to rent a bike, you have to be at least 18 years old. Don't forget to bring your passport or driverâ€™s license. Younger participants need to be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Riding Rules

All the renters have to stick to traffic rules, signs, laws, and regulations during the riding trip. It is advisable not to use smartphones or cameras..

Helmets

Additionally, we provide all our clients with mandatory helmets fitted right to your size. Be careful on the road!

Returning Equipment

Return our bike or gear at least 15 minutes prior to our closing time to check it out. We usually work till 6pm in spring and 7pm in summer.
